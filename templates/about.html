About PSRA

The Pharmaceutical Studies and Research Association (PSRA) is a dynamic student-led organization at the University of Khartoum, Faculty of Pharmacy. Founded with the vision of bridging the gap between academic learning and real-world application in pharmaceutical sciences, PSRA serves as a platform for students to engage in cutting-edge research, foster collaborations, and contribute to advancements in healthcare.

Our mission is to cultivate a culture of scientific inquiry and innovation among pharmacy students. We provide opportunities for hands-on research experience, professional development, and community engagement. Through partnerships with industry, academia, and healthcare organizations, we aim to equip our members with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the evolving field of pharmacy.

PSRA is affiliated with the Faculty of Pharmacy Students' Association (FPSA), ensuring alignment with broader student interests and institutional goals. Together, we strive to make a meaningful impact on pharmaceutical education and research in Sudan and beyond.
